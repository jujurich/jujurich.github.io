Pop Art Celebrities

I used Steve Harvey because he is one of the realest celebrities, he doesn't give into fame. He stays to himself.

Steve is a television host mainly for family feud. I choose the colors for my subject's face by taking the color

of his face and painting it with a brush. I used the swatches, crop tool, paint tool.


Fantastic Beasts

I came up with the animal I created by thinking of what can fit best. The animal can fly and he's quick and strong,

like a running back. I wanted an animal with a big body, so I chose a panda. I wanted an animal with an intimidating face,

so I chose a cheetah, and an animal with large wings, so I picked a vulture. I turned it into a hybrid by making the most

essential part of the animals and putting all of that together to make the strongest.
